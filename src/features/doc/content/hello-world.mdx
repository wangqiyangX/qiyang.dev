This is the first post on qiyang.dev.

This site is my personal home on the web. I use it to collect software projects,
engineering notes, and small UI experiments that are worth keeping around. It is
also where I can make my work visible in a way that feels closer to how I think:
practical, iterative, and focused on details that make interfaces more reliable.

qiyang.dev is adapted from
[chanhdai.com](https://github.com/ncdai/chanhdai.com), an excellent portfolio,
blog, and shadcn registry site created by ncdai. I am keeping the original
registry idea and the overall product-oriented structure, while replacing the
sample content with my own writing, links, components, and project information.

The direction is simple:

- write about frontend engineering and product interfaces
- document reusable components as they become useful
- keep the site small enough to maintain, but complete enough to grow with my
  work

Hello, qiyang.dev.
